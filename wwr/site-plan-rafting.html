Style Guide

Color Palette

Our company apply the following color Palette

  • River Blue (#00A4E4): This shade represents the color of a flowing river and conveys a sense of calmness and tranquility.
  • Sunshine Yellow (#FFD166): A bright and cheerful yellow reminiscent of the sun, adding warmth and energy to the palette.
  • Forest Green (#228B22): This deep green shade represents the lush foliage along the riverbanks and creates a connection to nature.
  • Safety Orange (#FF5733): This bold and attention-grabbing orange can be associated with safety gear and adds a pop of color to the palet
  • White Water Foam (#FFFFFF): Use white to represent the frothy whitecaps and rapids, creating contrast in your design.
