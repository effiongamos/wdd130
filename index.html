Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa, it is the most populous country in Africa. Nigeria borders Niger in the north, chad in the northeast, cameroon in the east and benin in the west. Nigeria is a Federal republic comprising 36 states and the Federal Capital territory, where the capital, Abuja is located. the largest city in Nigeria is Lagos. Nigeria is a multinational state inhabited by more than 250 ethnic groups speaking 500 distinct languages, all identifying with a wide variety of cultures.
